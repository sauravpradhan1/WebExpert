Digital Marketing



A website is an individual component within a digital marketing strategy, and which is complimentary to all other avenues that lead towards it and invite your customers to engage with you.


The more social media opportunities that you provide yourself will increase the probability of your potential customers finding you. We can help you set up professional (business) social media accounts and guide you on how to maintain and work with them. These include: Facebook, Twitter, Pintrest, Linked In, YouTube, Vimeo. We can also integrate these accounts with your website, feed tweets to your website and allow website visitors to tweet, like or pin from your individual website pages.


We additional provide proprietary email campaign software (dmail), that allows our clients to send mass marketing (designed) emails to their prospective target audiences.


Digital Marketing requires commitment and full time attention. If you are unable to find the time or assign an individual to this task, we are happy to be delegated to and engage with our clients regularly to gather marketing information before posting about it.


Web & Email Hosting



As a value added service we provide web and email hosting services to a number of our clients and are equally happy to provide this service to companies and individuals that fall outside of our web design portfolio.

For companies that we develop websites and content management systems for, some of the additional benefits of us providing hosting are that we are able to access a database and website files very quickly without needing to refer to a third party with a requirement to adjust settings and or change permissions within a web server.

The prospect of website and email hosting can often be bewildering to many individuals who do not wish to understand the technology, let alone have to fathom out how to configure server settings and set up email accounts. Our aim therefore is to take the pain of setting this up away from our clients, do everything that is required and then provide unlimited telephone and email support for whenever we are required to assist, which can often be to set up additional email addresses or adjust spam settings for an email account, but also help clients set up email accounts to collect on via an email client programme such as Outlook or Mac mail.

A number of organisations have email exchanges set up to distribute emails within offices or to remote computers. We are experienced at dealing with this type of solution and are very happy to work closely with appointed IT personnel in order to ensure that everything is set up to run smoothly and as seamlessly as possible.
